# Individual Contributor License Agreement (ICLA)

> Draft policy document for repository governance.

By submitting contributions to this repository, the contributor agrees that:

1. They own the submitted work or have sufficient rights to submit it.
2. The contribution may be distributed under current and future repository licensing models.
3. The project maintainer may sublicense, relicense, and distribute the contribution as part of commercial or non-commercial distributions of the project.
4. The contribution is provided without warranty.

## Operational Rule

- External contributors must provide DCO sign-off in commits.
- For accepted long-term external contributors, ICLA acceptance is required before merge authorization.
