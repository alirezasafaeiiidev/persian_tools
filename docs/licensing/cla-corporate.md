# Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

> Draft policy document for organization-backed contributions.

By submitting contributions on behalf of an organization, the authorized representative confirms:

1. The organization has rights to submit the contribution.
2. The contribution can be redistributed under current and future project licensing models.
3. The project maintainer may sublicense and relicense the contribution for commercial and non-commercial project distributions.
4. Contributors listed by the organization are authorized for contribution under this agreement.

## Operational Rule

- External corporate contributions must include DCO sign-off on commits.
- CCLA acceptance is required before merge authorization for organization-backed contributions.
